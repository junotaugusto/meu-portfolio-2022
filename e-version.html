About me

My name is Junot Augusto, I'm 40 years old and I'm from Londrina, Paraná. I have a degree in Social Communication - Journalism from Metropolitana IESB since 2006.
I started my career in 2007 as a Press Officer and later, in 2009, I migrated to the sales area, commercializing textile, hygiene, thermoplastic, commodities, among other products. During almost 10 years in the field, I held several positions, including Sales Representative and External Salesman, always seeking to improve professionally.
In 2019, I returned to my field of education and started working as a freelance journalist, producing newspapers for gated communities, press releases for public relations companies, and, about two years later, as a journalist and production coordinator at AlmA Londrina Rádio Web, producing and hosting radio programs.
In addition, since 2020, I have been training in the information technology area, for which I have always had an interest, and I am currently studying Cybersecurity. I am looking for new job opportunities, both in the areas I have already worked in and in this new information technology segment, which I am exploring and dedicating myself to every day. I am always seeking learning and new challenges for my personal and professional development.
